Water-based paints are the most popular type of paint and are available in both matte and glossy finishes. They are easy to clean up with water and are the best choice for children's rooms or for people who have allergies because they don't contain any harsh chemicals. However, they aren't as durable as oil-based paints and can't be used on surfaces that will be exposed to moisture. Oil-based paints are more durable than water-based paints and come in both matte and glossy finishes. They also have a higher color saturation than water-based paints, which means that they can create a richer look. However, they are more difficult to clean up than water-based paints and can also cause allergic reactions in some people.
